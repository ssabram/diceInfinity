üé≤ Gameplay

  • What types of dice can I roll with this app?
    You can roll from d2 (coin flip) to a d5000.
  • How many dice can I roll?
    The app supports from one to a hundred dice per dice set.
  • Can I roll different types of dice at once?
    Yes, up to four different types. Example: 1d5000 + 25d100 + 3d4 + 2d2.
  • Can I roll and keep some dice for re-rolls?
    Yes. Tap on rolled dice and select "Keep" to omit them from the next roll.
  • Is this app suitable for tabletop RPG games?
    Yes, it's designed for many dice-based RPGs.
  • Can I use it for board and casual games?
    Yes, with dice set customization if needed.
