Common Questions

What types of dice can I roll with this app?
You can roll many dice with many number of sides — from d2 (coin flip) to d5000.

Can I roll multiple dice at once?
Yes, you can roll up to four different types of dice at once. Dice sets such as "1d5000 + 25d100 + 3d4 + 2d2" are possible.

Can I play a game where I roll dice, keep some, and re-roll the rest?
Yes, this style of play is supported. You can individually choose which rolled dice to keep and then re-roll only the unkept dice for each round. Tap on the rolled dice and select "Keep" to omit that rolled dice from the next set roll.

How do I create, edit, save or delete dice sets?
Open the dice set editor via the edit icon or a pop-over menu in the set view. To edit a dice set, a custom keyboard shows allowing edits.

Can I see a history or log of my previous rolls?
Yes, the app keeps a log of your rolls so you can review your results anytime.

How do you export the dice roll history of this app?
All dice roll history can be copied into the iOS clipboard to paste into other apps that accept text from the clipboard.

Are the dice rolls randomized fairly?
All dice rolls are simulated where there is equal probability of each dice face showing in a given simulated dice.

How is the dice roll determined?
The app uses Apple's built-in iOS randomization functions to generate each roll.

Does the app work offline without internet access?
Yes, the app is fully functional offline and requires no internet connection.

Can I change the app’s theme or appearance?
You can switch between a light blue theme and a bold dark blue theme. Tap on the theme's thumbnail image under the View tab to change themes.

Does the app include sound effects during rolls?
Yes, realistic dice sound effects enhance the experience during animations.

Is this app suitable for tabletop RPG games?
Yes, it's designed to work with many games that uses dice.

Can I adjust the size of the views?
Yes, you can resize the dice view by using the min/max button in the upper-left corner. You can also adjust the tab position by dragging one of the two-headed arrow icons between the panels.

Does the app support both vertical and horizontal screen orientations?
Yes, the app is fully responsive and supports both portrait and landscape modes on all supported iOS devices.

Are there subscriptions or in-app purchases?
No subscriptions are required. Some advanced features may be available as one-time purchases or subscriptions in future updates.

Is there a way to avoid animations?
Yes, tap on the bar above the large dice graphic. This produces a "quick roll" without any animations.

Can I disable the shake action, roll vibrations or sounds?
Yes, you can toggle shake action, roll vibrations and sound effects under the User tab.

Will my dice roll data be shared or collected by the app?
No. The app does not collect any personal data. Only anonymous statistics from the App Store are viewed.

Is the app available for Android devices?
We truly appreciate your interest! At this time, the app is available exclusively for iOS devices. We are always evaluating future platforms based on user demand.
